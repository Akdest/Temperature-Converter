How to
Convert?

Converting between Celsius (°C) and Fahrenheit (°F):


To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the formula:
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32


To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, use the formula:
°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9


Converting Fahrenheit (°F) to Kelvin (K):


To convert Fahrenheit to Kelvin, use the formula:
K = (°F + 459.67) × 5/9


These conversion formulas allow you to easily switch between different temperature scales for your specific needs.
